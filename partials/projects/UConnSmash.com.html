UConnSmash.com

Nov 2014 - May 2015, Aug 2015 - Oct 2015

UConnSmash.com was one of my earliest web projects, and initially was just an information hub about the Melee club events at UConn.

Since then, the site has evolved to provide personalized information to each participant in the club, as well as establishing a skill based ranking system based on the Microsoft Trueskill algorithm.

Tournament results are aggregated into our MySQL database with PHP and then run through the ranking algorithm, we then provide a front end with AngularJS and Twitter Bootstrap to show who ranks where.

There are also interfaces for individuals to see their statistics such as their history of tournament rankings and the wins and losses of each individual set played.
