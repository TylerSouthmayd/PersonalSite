NAME
       mv - move
SYNOPSIS
       mv [ARGUMENT] [OPTION]
       mv [ARGUMENT1] [OPTION] [ARGUMENT2]
       mv [ARGUMENT1] [ARGUMENT2] [OPTION]
DESCRIPTION
       Used in a literal sense - applies the specified option to the content piece argument to reposition items on the view.
ARGUMENTS / Options
       All movable components have the following options:
            --up or -u, --down or -d, --top or -t, and --bottom or -b
       Movable components:
            project
                projectheader
                tylersouthmayd.com
                raspberrypi
                uconnsmash.com
                chinook
                htmleditor
            navbar
            intro
